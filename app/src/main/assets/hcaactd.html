Close a Term Deposit Account (HCAACTD)

Invoke the Menu HCAACTD and follow the below procedure for closing the Term Deposit Account of a Customer.
FunctionZ - Close
A/c. IDEnter the Account Number or select using the search
Closure Value DateCurrent date will be auto populated by the system
Withdrawal Amt. (Principal)Outstanding deposit amount will be displayed
Click on Go button
A/c Information Tab
Verify the details displayed
Closure Details Tab
Close ModeSelect R - Repayment A/c Only option
Repayment A/c. IDEnter the Repayment Account which the closure proceeds to be credited
Visit Closure Exceptions Tab
Click Submit. User can view the details of the account by expanding the respective Tabs
Click Ok button.
