Reprint Passbook (HPBPR)

In Finacle 10, once the passbook is printed, for reprinting the already printed transactions, the date, from which reprint is required, needs to be resetted using HPBPR Menu. Invoke the menu and follow the below procedure.
FunctionSelect B - Reset
A/c. IDEnter the Account Number
From DateEnter the From Date as applicable
To DateWill be auto populated as current date
Click on Accept
The transactions will be displayed in the grid. Select the Next Reset Date from which the passbook needs to be reprinted.
Click on Submit
