Introduction

In Finacle 7, CUMM Menu was used for all types of Customer ID related activities. Also In Finacle 7, the classification of Customer into Individual and Non Individual was done based on the Constitution Code of the Customer. In Finacle 10, Customer ID has been renamed as Customer Information File (CIF) Another significant change in Finacle 10, is the classification of Customer Information File (CIF) into the following ? Individual (Retail) ? Non Individual (Corporate) The data related to Retail and Corporate customers are captured using dedicated menus. Information gathered through this menu is entirely different and specific to the type of customer (Individual/Non Individual). Please note that once a CIF is opened, swapping of individual Customer ID to Non Individual Customer ID and vice versa is not permitted. Following are the Finacle 10 menus for Customer ID operations:

Create CIF CIF Retail >> New Entity >> Customer
