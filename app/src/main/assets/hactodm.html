Introduction

In Finacle 7, Temporary Overdraft was granted to customers using ACTODM and verification was done using ACTODMAU Menu. In Finacle 10, TOD related activities can be carried out using the following Menus.
HACTODMFor granting/verification of TOD in an account
HACTODIFor inquiring TOD granted in an account
HTODRPTOD Register Print

Creation of TOD

For Granting TOD in an account, Invoke the Menu HACTODM and follow the below mentioned procedure.
FunctionModify
A/c. IDEnter the Account Number
Click on Go Button
Click on Add New Record Button
Grant DateEnter the Current date
Advance Amt.Enter the TOD Amount
InterestSelect A/c. Level Interest
Click on Accept
Expiry DateExpiry Date will be auto populated
Penalty DatePenalty Date will be auto populated
Advance TypeSelect the appropriate option
Permitted BySelect the User ID
NotesEnter the Remarks
Click on Submit

Verification of TOD

For verifying the TOD granted, Invoke the Menu HACTODM and follow the below mentioned procedure.
FunctionVerify
A/c. IDEnter the Account Number or select using search
Click on Go Button
Click on Grant Date hyperlink. Details of TOD granted will be displayed
Ensure the correctness of the data entered and Click on Accept
Click on Submit
