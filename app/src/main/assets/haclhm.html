Introduction

In Finacle 10, the existing procedure Review, Renewal and Extension of validity period of CC/OD accounts will continue as such.
The following Menus are available in Finacle 10 for carrying out the limit maintenance.
1. LAR Loan Application Register
2. HACLHM Account Limit History Maintenance
3. ACSTATUS 87 Review Confirmation (CBS Annex Module)

Sanction Limit Modification

For Modifying the Sanction Limit of an account invoke the Menu HACLHM and follow the below procedure.
